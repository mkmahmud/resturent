FAQ

How far in advance can I make a reservation?

Reservations are released one month in advance to the calendar date at 12:00 AM Pacific Daylight Time.

Can dietary restrictions be accommodated?

We are able to make accommodations for common dietary restrictions (for example: vegetarian, pescatarian and can remove shellfish, nuts, or gluten from our menus) with at least 72 hours of advance notice. Please know that it may not be possible for us to accommodate all dietary restrictions and allergies. Please email info@quincerestaurant.com with any specific allergy questions or concerns.

What does my purchase of a reservation include?

Your purchase includes your food menu, service and taxes.

Supplements to the menu and alcoholic beverages (if you do not choose the wine pairing in advance), are available for purchase at the time of your experience for an additional expense.

What are the taxes and service charge applied to my bill?

For all dining room reservations, a 20% service charge will be added your bill as well as a 5% surcharge for San Francisco employee mandates. The entirety of these charges is retained by Cotogna, of which 100% is distributed to all non- management employees in the form of wages and benefits. We believe this compensation model creates equity, stability, and constancy for all employees across the kitchen and dining room.

Any gratuity paid on the evening of your reservation is at the discretion of each guest and is not required.

What Covid precautions are being taken?

At Quince, we update our policies to reflect the most recent guidelines from the CDC and the City of San Francisco.

PROOF OF VACCINATION REQUIRED

As of Friday, August 20, 2021, proof of COVID-19 vaccination will be required for patrons wishing to eat indoors at restaurants in San Francisco. Upon entering the restaurant, you will be asked to show your vaccination card; a legible photocopy of the card, or a photograph of the card on your cellphone, and/or a Digital COVID-19 CA state vaccine record QR code . We will also continue requiring masks and social distancing throughout the restaurant per current City health mandate requirements. Thank you for your cooperation.

Please visit sf.gov/topics/coronavirus-covid-19 for the latest city mandates.

We prioritize your wellbeing and are taking the following additional steps for both our indoor and outdoor dining. The design of the Quince dining room inherently facilitates social distancing with generous spacing between our tables. Quince has installed a GPS Needlepoint Bipolar Ionization system that uses air purification technologies to continuously reduce pathogens and improve air quality.
